About Me

I'm Margot ter Maat, a visual developer, concept artist and animator, currently studying animation at Willem de Kooning Academy in Rotterdam.

I specialize in bringing stories to life through thoughtful character design, immersive environments, and detailed prop development. My work focuses on the pre-production phase of animation, where ideas transform into visual blueprints that serve the narrative.
